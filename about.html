ABOUT


SatTracker allow you to see satellites orbiting around our Earth. You can show them by their name, country, launching year and their weight. You also have additional informations when you click on them.


We get positions of satelittes from NASA database and convert them into Latitude, Longitude and Altitude. Some of them orbit around Earth, some other are geostationary. They provide informations for weather, communications, science, GPS etc.


Sat Tracker was proudly made during an intensive week at HETIC !
